First-Time Setup: Allow Your Microphone

  1. Click the Windows Start button, then open Settings.
  2. Go to Privacy & Security → Microphone.
  3. Make sure "Microphone access" is turned ON.
  4. Also turn on "Let desktop apps access your microphone".
  5. Come back here and click Start Recording. When Chrome asks permission, click Allow.
